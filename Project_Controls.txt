---Camera---
W,A,S,D = Moving forward, left, down, right
Left Shift = Move camera directly down
Space = Move camera directly up
Left click and hold = Move camera around

---Render Controls---
Press 1 = Shows joints and wireframe of model
Press 3 = Shows full mesh of teddy

---Light Controls---
NumPad 2,4,6,8 = Changes directional light to show it working on mesh
